Madron Feast Week

 

Madron Feast Week - Sunday 29th November to Friday 4th December

 

Sunday 29th November

11am - United Feast Service at the Parish Church.

6pm - United Feast Service at Madron chapel.

 

Monday 30th November

11am - Meet of the Western Hunt.

11am - Feast Bazaar in Landithy Hall. Refreshments available between 11am and 2pm including hot and cold drinks, cakes, hot soups and rolls.

 

Tuesday 1st December

7.30pm - Feast Feast Bingo at Landithy Hall - £1.

 

Wednesday 2nd December

10am to 12pm - Charity coffee morning & bring and buy sale in aid of Age Concern in the Chapel's Sunday school room.

7pm - Killer Pool & Darts tournament at the King William IV pub. £1 entry.

 

Thursday 3rd December

7.30pm - Feast Concert in Landithy Hall. Tickets are available from the Madron Shop. Adults £2.50, Children £1.

 

Friday 4th December

7.30pm - Feast Concert in Landithy Hall.
