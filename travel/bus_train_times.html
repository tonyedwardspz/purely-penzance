Penzance Bus and Train Timetables

 

Bus Information

The West Cornwall bus network connects Penzance to the rest of the area, with services operated by two bus companies. During the summer months a circular route (502) runs from Penzance, travelling around West Cornwall via the costal towns and hot spots.

- Penzance Bus Station
Station Approach, Penzance, Cornwall

- Bus Times and Public Transport Planner
www.transportdirect.info

 

Train Information

Penzance's train station is the last stop on the national rail network. From here you can get connections to London, Liverpool, Glasgow, Cardiff as well as the UK's major cities.

- Penzance Train Station
Station Approach, Penzance, Cornwall

- Public Transport Planner
www.transportdirect.info
