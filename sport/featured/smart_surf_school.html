Smart Surf

 

The Blue Lagoon, Sennen Cove, Penzance, Cornwall, TR19 7DF
Phone - 01736 871817
www.sennensurfschool.com

 

Our motto is to enjoy and our aim is to provide our students with a safe, personalised and correct introduction to the art of surfing. All lessons are conducted by professional surfers.

We love what we do. We are very a friendly bunch, yet appreciate our responsibilities in tutoring. Professionalism is our watch word whenever tutoring, either the young or old, newbies or pros.

The Smart Surf School in Cornwall was established in the year 2000 and has gone from strength to strength ever since. Our students tell us they enjoy the relaxed, friendly way we operate, whilst feeling confident and safe with our small tutor to student ratio, never more than 7, which allows us to keep a close eye on everyone.
