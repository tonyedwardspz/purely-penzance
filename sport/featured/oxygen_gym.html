  • 4 Market Jew Street, Penzance
  • 01736 333831

Details

Imagine starting your day with some gentle exercise whilst witnessing the spectacle of the sun rising out of the sea in front of you. Imagine then continuing your day of well-being in an Infra-Red Sauna overlooking St. Michael’s Mount. This is just the beginning of your journey with Oxygen, which is located on the top floors of a beautiful old granite building in the heart of Penzance. The club has undergone a radical transformation since its launch 5 years ago. We have developed the business into a one-stop health centre with a team of highly talented Personal Trainers and Therapists. It is home to an extensive variety of services, which are essential to a healthy lifestyle:

  • State-of-the-art Gymnasium
  • Sports Massage
  • Chiropractic Clinic
  • Colonic Hydrotherapy
  • Kinesiology Clinic
  • Beauty Studio
  • Aromatherapy Massage
  • Craniosacral Therapy
  • Nutrition Therapy
  • Professional Yoga and Pilates Studio
  • Traditional and Infra-Red Saunas

Oxygen has a friendly and relaxed atmosphere, which is all part of our non-intimidation philosophy. We strive to make every visit to the club, especially training, a pleasurable and sociable experience. Pride is taken in providing a personalised service, and high standards of hygiene and cleanliness are a priority.

In an innovative move, Oxygen is one of the UK’s leading health and fitness clubs offering a pioneering ‘No Contract’ Membership – a concept that is virtually unheard of in the leisure industry. Our ethos is to maintain good healthy relationships with our clients at all costs. Oxygen is a special centre for all age groups and with affordable prices. Our team’s wealth of knowledge is crucial in helping people take care of their minds and bodies in a harmonious environment.
