Touch Gloves, Thai Kick Boxing

- Touch Gloves Muay Thai Kickboxing

  • www.touchgloves.co.uk
  • 07974 333489

Details

Touch Gloves is the no. 1 club for fitness and self defence. Their reliable and qualilfied instructors will teach you the devestating art of Thai Boxing in a safe & friendly environment.

Classes cater for all ages and abilities and will improve fitness, relieve stress and increse flexibility and self confidence. The friendly atmosphere at the club, located in Branwell's Mill, is second to none offering a no attitude, no intimidation & no bullying policy. Seperate woman & children classes are ran.

Muay Thai & Boxing techniques are practiced on pads, not people and a qualified first aider is always on hand. A relaxation are with cusioned seats & a plasma screen allow classes to be viewed before you take part.
