Details

Hardcore Jeet Kune Do Southwest offer lessons in the martial art, as practiced by Bruce Lee, in a safe and friendly environment.

Classes cater for all abilities and will improve fitness, relieve stress and increase flexibility and self confidence.

At Jeet Kune DO you will learn to implement an array of strikes including strikes with; fists, feet, legs, knees, elbows, head, palms and forearms utilizing one of the five ways of attack. You will also practice sensitivity drill, grappling, chokes and locks.

 

About Jeet Kune Do

When Bruce Lee moved to the United States of America he opened a school in Seattle, Washington where he practiced and taught a modified Gung Fu. 

The second school he opened was in Oakland. It was during this period that he had a fight with another practitioner from another art. The fight did not go as well as he wanted it to. This caused Bruce to re assess and this led to him modifying his system to improve its effectiveness and called it JKD.

 

About the Instructor

Since a very young age Robert Lay trained in an array of martial arts. In 2001 he started training in Jeet Kune do, Kali and Silat extensively. In 2006 the 'student became the master' when he started teaching classes.

In 2008 Robert was lucky enough to train with Sifu Lamar Davis, who he now teaches Jeet Kune Do under.

These days Robert almost exclusively trains and teaches Jeet Kune Do in order to master the most effective combat system in the world.
