Public & Government Services in Penzance & Penwith


Penzance and the surrounding towns & villages falls under the administrative jurisdiction of Cornwall Council. Penzance is represented in Parliament as part of the St. Ives Constituency, headed up by Andrew George MP. Penwith is part of the EuropeanParliamentary constituency for the South West Region of England. From April 2009, Penwith and the rest of Cornwall has been served by One Cornwall Council.
