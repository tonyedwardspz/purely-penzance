Dentists

Penzance has nine dental practices situated in the town, with many more in the neighbouring towns and villages. Even though there are many Dentists in the area, only a select few accept NHS patients, with the largest being 'Penwith Dental Practice'. The 'Bellair Dental Clinic' Operates a drop in service for those needing an emergency dentist.
