Recharge your batteries, Nourish your Body & Soul within the beautiful landscape of Cape Cornwall by the sea, near Penzance

Delphiris, offers 'Healing Touch' a variety of Training & Treatments -tailored to the needs of the individual- such as Reiki & Indian Head Massage; Ocean Therapy (using the essence of the Ocean to facilitate positive changes; this includes 'Aqua Healing Touch - releasing fear of water'), Chavutti-Thai Massage.

We now also offer Training courses on Malta. Please find more details on my website."
