College's & Further Education in Penzance & Cornwall

 

For those who have just left secondary school and wish to enter further education, there is no need to leave Cornwall to study, even at a University level. All of the major colleges offer higher education courses. In fact, Cornwall College is the UK's largest Further Education provider, and Truro College is one of the highest rated in the South of England.

Penwith, Truro & Cornwall Colleges all offer courses aimed at mature students who wish to re-enter education. Who knows, you may even be eligible for ALG.
