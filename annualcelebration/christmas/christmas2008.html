Christmas in Penzance, Newlyn & Mousehole, 2008

 

Christmas in Penzance

The Christmas lights this year will be switched on by the Mayor on Saturday 29th November at 4pm in the wharfside. The light display will be along Market Jew Street, stretching to the top of Causeway Head and originating in the Wharfside Shopping Centre.

Saturday 29th November

  • 10am - Puppet Show - Wharfside Shopping Centre
  • 1pm - Puppet Show - Wharfside Shopping Centre
  • Hammered Steel Band - 2pm - Wharfside Shopping Centre
  • Christmas Lights Switch on - 4pm - Wharfside Shopping Centre
  • Hammered Steel Band - 5pm - Wharfside Shopping Centre

Late night shopping

Late night shopping in Penzance will begin on Thursday 4th December and continues throughout the month every Thursday night.

 

Christmas in Newlyn & Mousehole

  • Newlyn Christmas lights switch on - Friday 12th December at 7.30pm
  • Mousehole Christmas lights switch on - Saturday 13th December and 7.30pm.
    (Roads closed from 5.30)

It is highly recommended that you do not take your car to Newlyn or Mousehole on these nights. The streets will be packed with people, and parking will be near impossible.

Oats Travel & Mounts Bay Coaches will be operating coach tours of the Christmas displays. Get in contact via the travel page.

British International, located on Eastern Green, will be operating 10 minute helicopter flights over Newlyn & Mousehole. This is the best way to see the festive lights. Contact British International Helicopters or the Tourist Information Centre for more info.

 

The Christmas light dates for local towns & villages

  • Hayle - Friday 28th November
  • Helston - Friday 28th November
  • Penzance - Saturday 29th November
  • Angarrack - Saturday 6th December
  • Camborne - Saturday 6th December
  • Brege - Saturday 6th December
  • Gulval - Monday 8th December
  • St. Just - Friday 12th December
  • Leedstown - Friday 12th December
  • Newlyn - Friday 12th December
  • Mousehole - Saturday 13th December
  • Goldsithney - Monday 15th December
