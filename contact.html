Contact with the Purely Penzance Team

 

Want to place an advert? Wish to add a link to your web site? Need to update your business details? Get in touch.

If you need to talk to any of the Purely Penzance team, please do not hesitate to drop us a message through our Facebook Profile. If you would like to talk to us, send your phone number in the message and we will get in touch at your convenience.

Please note;

  • We do not offer further information on services in the area
  • We do not offer brochures
  • We do no provide an accommodation booking service
  • We do not provide travel information

If you do require these services you should contact the Tourist Information Centre who will be happy to help.
