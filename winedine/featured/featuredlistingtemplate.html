Details

Sunny Bank is a small friendly family run hotel offering comfortable accommodation all year round. We are situated on the main A30 as you enter Sennen on the left hand side of the road.

A great location for visitors to the Lands End peninsula, literally only 1½ miles from breathtaking views at Lands End itself.

Sennen cove is minutes away with golden sands providing safe bathing and surfing having been awarded blue flat status. For the budding surfers out there, Sennen's beaches offers some of the UK's best surf, in a breath taking and easy to access location.

The Minack theatre is approx. 10 minute drive away offering afternoon and evening performances from world class performance groups. Nearby Lands End provides an excellent family day out, with the Airport close by offering scenic flights and day trips to the Isles of Scilly.


We have en-suite facilities and offer tea/coffee-making trays & TVs in all rooms as well as radio alarm clocks.

Breakfast is served between 8-9am in the dining room where we provide a hearty full English breakfast to start your day - "veggie option" is available.

  • Village location only minutes from Sennen Beach
  • Sea/country views from all rooms
  • Licensed bar
  • Ten minutes from the world renowned "Minack" open air theatre
  • Lockable storage for bikes/surfboards
  • No smoking
  • En-suite rooms
  • Sorry no pets
