Pets Boutique
Your friendly & independent family run pet shop

 

Pets Boutique has been established in the seaside town of Penzance for well over 30 years. Its main road location in Market Jew Street allows easy access from the town railway & bus stations as well as the main harbour car park

During its history it has always been a family run store, and for the last 5 years it has been owned by Kevin & Nina and their two Sons. They have strived to improve their service to both the local community and visitors alike, without loosing sight of the things that are most important; friendly service, great customer relations, value for money, prompt delivery service and always endeavouring to find what you want as quickly as possible if said item is not in stock.

The shop is well stocked, but on occasion we are asked for items which we do not hold on the premises. We have a direct link to our suppliers with over 18,000 pet products, viewable on on computers in store. We take 4 deliveries a week, meaning all ordered items are generally available within 48 hours of ordering.

  • Free Delivery in Penzance Area (minimum order £10)
  • All major food brands supplied - small bags to large
  • Larger sacks of food ordered on request
  • Vast array of pet cages, hutches and fish tanks available
  • Extensive range of flea & veterinary products
  • Bedding, collars, leads, clothing and pet treats
  • Large range of accessories & toys for pets of all shapes and sizes
  • Aquatic goods including lights, pumps, filters and much more

If you are planning to visit the West of Cornwall, why not contact us in advance to order your pet food. You can collect it from our conveniently located store or have it delivered to your holiday residence (small reservation fee applies)
