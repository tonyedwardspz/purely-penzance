Morrab Studio Gift Shop

 

- Morrab Studio (01736 362191)
1-2 Stewart Building, Upper Morrab Road, Penzance, TR18 2QU
https://www.morrabstudio.co.uk/

 

Morrab Studio Giftshop was established in 1950, and are specialists in fine bone china and cut crystal glass. The offer offer a picture framing and restoration service, and are suppliers of high quality giftware, collectables, pictures and prints. Morrab Studio also offer a wide selection of print reproductions from the Newlyn School of Art, and sell an array of original artwork.

Morrab Studio specialises in Fine bone China Tableware, Crystal Stemware and quality Cutlery, stocking brands such as Waterford, Spode, Wedgwood, Arthur Price, Royal Worcester, Villeroy & Boch. A wide range of Giftware and Collectables including ranges from Moorcroft Pottery, Swarovski, Crown Derby, Country Artists, Hummel and Lladro.

Morrab Studios also offers an array of original art and Fine Art Prints, plus offer a inhouse bespoke Picture Framing Service. They specialise in Prints from the Newlyn School of Art, including Stanhope Forbes, Walter Langley amongst others.
