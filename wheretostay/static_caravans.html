Static Caravans in and around Penzance & Penwith

 

Once you’ve tried a static caravan holiday, you could find yourself converted, like many other holiday makers before you!

People often shy away from static caravan holidays, worried about lack of space or facilities, but anyone who has stayed in a static caravan recently will tell you that it’s an affordable, spacious and comfortable way to spend your holiday.

Some caravan parks have on-site shops, restaurants and entertainment, offering you everything you need in a dedicated area and allowing you to really enjoy and explore the beautiful area of Penwith.
