Enjoy England Tourist Board and Penzance

 

Enjoy England is the official tourist board for England and part of VisitBritain. There mission, to grow the value of tourism throughout the English regions, throughout the year.

The Enjoy England Quality Rose reassures you before you check into your holiday accommodation that it will be just what you want, because it’s been checked out by independent assessors. The Quality Rose is the mark of England’s official, nationwide quality assessment scheme and is an independent, reliable, impartial assessment of quality covering just about every type of place you might want to stay. Think of the Quality Rose as your personal guarantee that your expectations will be met.
