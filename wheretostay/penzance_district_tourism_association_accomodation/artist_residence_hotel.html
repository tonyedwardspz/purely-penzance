Artist Residence

 

The Georgian House, Chapel Street, Penzance, TR18 4AW
Phone - 01736 365664
www.arthotelcornwall.co.uk

 

The Artist Residence is a boutique guesthouse in the historic heart of Central Penzance, situated within a Grade II listed 17th Century Georgian mansion on Chapel Street.

Each of the playful boutique hotel style rooms have been individually designed and decorated by Cornish & British artists. Choose one that appeals to your inner artist, from wacky street art murals to pastel sea-scapes, each room is totally unique.

Stay in your own private masterpiece with the comforts of top-notch bedding, en-suite bathrooms, free Wi-Fi, flat screen digital TV’s and eco-chic toiletries. All guests have access to the magnificent all-white room on the ground floor, which serves as a breakfast room, gallery, chill-out space and cafe.

 

The Cafe

Arts Kitchen is a cafe and events venue with a difference! Situated within the quirky gallery space, Arts Kitchen offers delicious breakfasts, brunch, lunch, home made cakes and afternoon tea's as well as a wide range of coffees, teas and soft drinks. Locally sourced, wholesome food made from scratch, focused around fresh seasonal ingredients dictate the menu with an exciting range of daily specials.
