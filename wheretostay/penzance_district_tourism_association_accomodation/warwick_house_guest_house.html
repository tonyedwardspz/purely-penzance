Warwick House Guest House

 

17 Regent Terrace, Penzance, Cornwall TR18 4DW
Phone - 01736 363881
www.warwickhousepenzance.co.uk

 

Warwick House is a charming Regency period Guest House built in Penzance in the 1830’s and now a listed building with superb views of Mount’s Bay. The Guest House enjoys an enviable position in Penzance with the promenade in front of the accommodation, whilst the quaint inns and restaurants of  historic Chapel Street lie behind the house.

Most of the guest bedrooms have stunning sea views and are impeccably presented and provide a high level of comfort, encouraging you to relax as soon as you arrive.

All rooms have TV/ DVD player (with complimentary film library), tea and coffee making facilities, mineral water, and many other little extras to make you feel instantly at home.

Breakfast are served in the spacious dining room and includes fare such as full english breakfasts, Cornish Muffins, Egs benedict and much more. Whenever possible, local produce is used including Sausages and Hogs pudding are from the award winning family butcher Lenterns.
