Penmorvah Guest House

 

61 Alexandra Road, Penzance, Cornwall, TR18 4LZ
Phone - 01736 363711
www.penmorvah.net

 

Penmorvah is a family run Guest House situated on a tree lined road in central Penzance. It is a short walk to Penlee Park, the Prommenade, the town centre as well as Penzance's many restaurants, gallerys and transport links.

All rooms are en-suite and have tea & coffee making facilities and are serviced daily. Towels are provided along with a Hair dryer. An ironing board and iron are available on request.
