Hotel Penzance

 

Britons Hill, Penzance, Cornwall TR18 3AE
Phone - 01736 363117
www.hotelpenzance.com

 

Welcoming, intimate and comfortable, the 4* townhouse Hotel Penzance is in an elegant, Edwardian building with only 25 individual rooms, many with views across the harbour and Mounts Bay. Set back from the town but only five minutes from the centre, Hotel Penzance is the ideal place to stay for leisure or business.

Whether you are staying for a few nights or just for one, the friendly team will take time and care to provide everything you need through attentive service, help with planning your day, tickets or onward bookings.

A Delicious breakfast is served in The Bay, the adjoining 2 AA Rosette Award winning restaurant. On offer is a selectio of freshly cooked breakfasts using local produce along side a buffet of favourites.
