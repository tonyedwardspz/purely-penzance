The Corner House

 

20 Marine Terrace, Penzance, TR18 4DL
Phone - 01736 351324
www.thecornerhousepenzance.co.uk

 

The Corner House have undertaken extensive refurbishment to ensure their guests have a comfortable and pleasant stay.The property dates back to 1848 and many of the rooms retain some original features.

A range of rooms are availble, some enjoy spectacular views of Mounts Bay. All rooms are Ensuite with Flatscreen TV / DVD / CD player, Wireless Internet, Toiletries and Hospitality tray.

Guests can take advantage of the spectacular views reaching to St Micheals Mount and beyond from their rooms or from the garden seating area.
