Living in Penzance & the Community

 

Penzance is the most South-Westerly town in Mainland Britain, with some of the longest hours of sunshine, and the mildest winters. These mild winters and long sunny days help to keep the locals happy(ish) all year long.

The laid back way of life within the community can be summed up by the Cornish Motto, 'Do it Dreckly'. This makes Penzance one of the nicest places to live in Britain, well deserving of the title 'Capital of Penwith'
