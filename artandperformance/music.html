Musical Groups & Bands in Penwith

 

The Penzance & greater Penwith area have given rise to a wide variety of musical artists, from melodic rockers to metal bands with a sprinkling of choirs for good measure. Many of the groups regularly perform at the areas live music venues as well as the summer festivals.

Penzance also boasts a number of record labels & industry standard recording studios
