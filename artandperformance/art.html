Art, Artists & Sculpture in Penwith

 

The exceptionally beautiful landscape of Penwith provides an excellent backdrop for artists. Hundreds of artists have come to Penwith looking for inspiration from the scenery and lifestyle afforded by the area.

As such Penzance and west cornwall have a thriving art scene with many artists holding exhibitions in the local art galleries.
