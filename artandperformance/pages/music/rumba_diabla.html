Rumba Diabla, Latin american dance trio

 

Rumba Diabla
www.rumbadiabla.co.uk

Rumba Diabla is a talented, lively trio of experienced musicians playing intricate, atmospheric Latin American dance music. They use many traditional instruments from those regions to produce an engaging, authentic, rich sound.

The group was formed 3 years ago and has 60 years of combine performing experience. They have received critical acclaim from the international musical community and consistent praise and warm appriciation from their wide ranging audiences.

To contact or book Rumba Diabla Graham Gynn via the website - www.rumbadiabla.co.uk
