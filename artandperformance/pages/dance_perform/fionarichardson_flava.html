Fiona Richardsons Big Dance Company

 

The Big Dance Company (01736 333444)
Chapel Street, Penzance, Cornwall, TR18 4BE
www.thebigdancecompany.com

The big dance Company offer classes in Freestyle (up to championship standard) Slow dance, Hip Hop, Break-dance, Cheerleading, Ballet, lyrical, and voice training.

They compete regularly at prestigious competitions and provide lots of exciting performance opportunities including an overseas programme in LA.

The company have a team of incredibly talented, passionate and creative teachers who are all dedicated to providing fun safe and high quality classes, which are available to children from 3 to adult professional level.

Flava

Flava are best known for reaching the semi finals of ITV’s ‘Britain’s Got Talent’ 2008. This has gained them national recognition and more importantly the opportunity to do what they love, which is to dance and perform.

Flava are best known for reaching the semi finals of ITV’s ‘Britain’s Got Talent’ 2008. This has gained them national recognition and more importantly the opportunity to do what they love, which is to dance and perform.

Said Simon Cowell: ‘The raw talent and the choreography are as good as anything I’ve seen in America.” They’re one of the best dance acts I’ve seen in this country. They’re terrific”
However, with the help and guidance of lead group member Kyle Richardson’s mother Fiona Richardson, Flava were a success in their own right before ‘Britain’s Got Talent’, not only winning numerous competitions but also representing England at the World Hip Hop Championships and the UK at the Dance Excellence Awards in Los Angeles.
