How does VistaPrint design and print such high quality products at such a low price?

In one word–technology. Historically, graphic design and custom printing have been hands-on,laborious processes for both the supplier and the customer. Inflated supplier costs resulted in exorbitant prices for the customer, even for print jobs in very small quantities.

Vista Print's proprietary technologies fully automate not only the manufacturing of printing, but also the manner in which orders are created and submitted. In a nutshell, they incur lower costs, which means that vistaprint's customers enjoy lower prices for higher quality printing.
